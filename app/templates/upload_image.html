FAQ

  • How do I get good results?

    High resolution, straight on, low noise

  • My face wasn't found!

    We use the open source dlib face detector to find faces.

  • Where did my glasses go?

    Not many characters from animated films where glasses so the model seems to have learnt to mostly remove them
